About Me

I am an Adjunct Lecturer of Computer Science and Engineering at Newcastle University College, Bangladesh since August 2017. Before that I was an Adjunct Lecturer of Computer Science and Engineering at International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh in Autumn 2016 (October 2016 - March 2017) semester. I received a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering from International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh in August 2016. My undergraduate research advisors were Professor Tanveer Ahsan and Lecturer (on leave) Md. Azher Uddin. I also worked with Professor Md Monirul Islam and Professor Muhammed Jamshed Alam Patwary in some projects. I have successfully completed several MOOCs on Coursera, edX and Stanford Lagunita - some of my certifications can be found at here (link 1, link 2, link 3, link 4). I love to learn new things and play and watch soccer.

Research Interest

I am interested in machine learning and its applications. During and post undergraduate studies, I have performed some research works on applied machine learning such as human behavior analysis, and social media analysis.

Competitions

  1. Kaggle Santander Customer Happiness Prediction Challenge: exploited hundreds of anonymized features to predict if a customer is satisfied or dissatisfied with their banking experience using ensemble learning. Leaderboard Position: top 30%. 2016

  2. Kaggle Restaurant Revenue Prediction Challenge: predicted annual restaurant sales of regional location from restaurant open date, location, city type, demographic data, real estate data, and commercial data. Leaderboard Position: top 25%. 2015

  3. Kaggle Otto Group Product Classification Challenge: classified 200,000 products into 9 major categories, e.g., fashion and electronics exploiting 93 numerical features representing counts of different events. Leaderboard Position: top 30\%. 2015

  4. Kaggle Higgs Boson Machine Learning Challenge: leveraged simulated data with features characterizing events detected by ATLAS experiment to distinguish between “tau tau decay of a Higgs boson” and “background” using tree-based models. 2014
