About Me

I am an Adjunct Lecturer of Computer Science and Engineering at Newcastle University College, Bangladesh since August 2017. Before that I was an Adjunct Lecturer of Computer Science and Engineering at International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh in Autumn 2016 (October 2016 - March 2017) semester. I received a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering from International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh in August 2016. My undergraduate research advisors were Professor Tanveer Ahsan and Lecturer (on leave) Md. Azher Uddin. I also worked with Professor Md Monirul Islam and Professor Muhammed Jamshed Alam Patwary in some projects. I have successfully completed several MOOCs on Coursera, edX and Stanford Lagunita - some of my certifications can be found at here . I love to learn new things and play and watch soccer.
